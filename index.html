My Vision

In life, learning is more than just education. It is the culmination of everything we get to experience. After two years of living life behind the screens, it is now time for the culture which, makes us who we are to be revitalised incorporating former glory with modern innovations. Agenda

TRANSPORT

  • Promote the use of cycles and shared electric bike services (like Yulu) for intra-campus travel

  • Reintroduction of buses running to and from nearby major transportation hubs

  • Push for the establishment of an official bus stop outside IITH

CLUBS

  • Establishment, maintenance and improvement of the new SAC room as the dedicated room for any student-group interaction especially for girls after 12

  • Promoting clubs to obtain funds for their own activities to make them more self reliant

  • Taking initiatives like making Club Brochures for better understanding of how the club works

CAMPUS INFRASTRUCTURE

  • PG21 and residents of Kapila will get preference for new hostel and UG20 for old hostels within a month of service

  • Restocking of umbrellas for use by students near acad blocks and mess during summers too

  • Provision of cold water dispensers in every hostel block and near the gym

  • Pushing for an open gym near the current football ground and adding calisthenic equipments too

DIGITAL INFRASTRUCTURE

  • Push for the installation of Network Boosters in hostels with network connectivity problems. Explore improved access to VPNs (without compromising on the enforced guidelines)

  • Set up systems to take feedback of changes through Gymkhana feedback forms and report back on its results through monthly reports

CAREER SPECIFIC

  • Assigning mentors to those appearing for internships and placements and extend this to students opting for further studies as well

  • We will work in collaboration with OCS to provide industry-relevant feedback to the sci-tech clubs so that the campus becomes more industry friendly and students get a lot of opportunities via working for clubs

STUDENT CENTRIC

  • Will aim to remove the 16 credit Additionals to Electives conversion cap for UG19 batch.

  • Improving the program of Alumni day by having more events and interaction opportunities

MISCELLANEOUS

  • Take initiative to allow PG students to be day scholars and not mandatorily campus residents

  • Revamp the allocation of course guide selection process to let the PG students choose between 2 random choice of professors

  • Promote collaboration between institutes like NIFT, BITS and IIITH for events like Elan to grow relevant networks and bring IITH into groups of relevance
